All about the Master Rangers

The master ranger is a team of teen aged kids who find powerful weapons and have to use them to save the world from upcoming threats. Each weapon is equal to the other. Thus there are no stronger or weaker weapon. For an example the red ranger has a sword but that does not mean it is better than the blue ranger's trident.
